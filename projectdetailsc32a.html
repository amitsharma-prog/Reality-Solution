AIPL Business Club Overview

The basic standards of a perfect commercial development are depends on site, accessibility from different location and peaceful ambience. Not only should a development be a construction of office spaces but should have a welcome and relaxed atmosphere for relaxed social interaction, like AIPL Business Club.


Be it a retail store & food court on ground floor integrated with the 14 level office spaces, it is the right place for them all. This commercial property is a resourcefully designed and purposefully planned development, which will offer excellent chance to place company in for MNC's, Corporate Houses, Call Centers, Banks and Institutes etc.


AIPL Business Club will be the most preferred destination of food lovers and shoppers. One can find a long chain of food options and shops on Ground Floors. The gourmet at this commercial development will be a proper demonstration of different flavors and an indication to a different cultural incorporation of the most preferred cuisines of different parts of the world. Wrapped in the most attractive appearance motivated by the global trends in the architectural field, it will exude opulence in peripheral design.


AIPL Business Club is located in Sector 62 Gurgaon on Corner Plot, on Golf Course Extension Road gives connectivity from Sohna Road & Gurgaon - Faridabad Road. This is a stand-alone development of three towers that one can never miss. With 4 level car parking, 2 basement and 7 lac sq. ft "Grade - A" office, at here you are on the top limit of managing your own business.


As expected from the AIPL Developments, this location is the center point of excellent infrastructure in Gurgaon and the prospects could only get brighter. If truth be told, this commercial development is a masterpiece in its true sense with wide open frontage and sighted from the long distance. The strategic value of AIPL Business Club is supreme as also the quality of the construction.

Facade Design

  • Facade is being designed based on solar & shadow analysis inputs from and green consultant using multiple features like double insulated glass, shading devices etc.to ensure better heat reduction and energy efficiency through reduction in the HVAC load of tenant area, thereby reducing their recurring cost.

  • Year round space temperature control

  • Use of the curtain wall glazing with vertical fins as a means of support to ensure that there is least obstruction in the glazing.

Green Building

  • Energy-efficient transport solutions. Provision for dedicated parking spaces equipped with electric charging stations for environment friendly electric cars.

  • 100% rain water harvesting on site.

  • To reduce pollution & land development impacts from automobiles use, bicycle storage & changing rooms will be developed in the building to encourage usc of such alternate non polluting means of transportation.

  • Proper & adequate parking capacity will be provided as per the by - laws.

  • More than 50% of the roof area will be landscaped.

  • 93% of the parking would be under covered roof.

Indoor Air Quality

  • Environment friendly low volatile organic compounds based paints, sealants & adhesives are proposed to maintain good indoor air quality. Further, composite wood & Agrifiber products would be used, which do not contain toxins & harmful chemicals like Urea Formaldehyde Resins.

  • 30% extra fresh air will be provisioned for to better counter the sick building syndrome & improve comfort level for the building occupants.

  • Thermal comfort of the building is being designed to provide a comfortable thermal enviromnent that promotes occupant's productivity & well-being.

Energy & Atmosphere

  • The proposed Building envelope is being designed to increase savings by reducing the load on air-conditioning with the application of insulation on roof.

  • Overall 16% of energy savings are estimated to be achieved over the expected energy consumption in conventional building systems.

  • Optimal lighting levels are proposed to be maintained in the building.

  • Highly efficient water-cooled screw chillers will be used.

  • Treated fresh air units will be installed in the building.

Material & Resources

  • 60% of the material used in the project would be manufactured & extracted regionally within a 400 kms. radius.

  • More than 40% of the materials would have recycled content in it.

  • The project will be implementing waste management plan and diverting more than 95% of construction waste to uses other than landfills.

Water Efficiency

  • 100% of waste water will be treated on-site at the STP which will be further used for:

    • Landscaping

    • Air-Conditioning

    • Washroom flushing

  • More than 20% reduction in potable water consumption is expected due to the use of low flow taps & flush fixtures in the toilets.

Extraordinary features

  • "Pay as per actual usage." This is the motto while planning all energy consuming systems with a tenant wise transparent metering model.

  • Highly secure & safe environment will be maintained in the building using latest fire & life safety system designs.

  • Low CAM model as air - conditioning will be metered and paid by tenant directly.

  • Building is proposed to be maintained by our in-house facility management company.

  • Vertical transportation is being planned and designed to achieve optimal waiting and travel time as per international standards - average waiting time of less than 30 seconds during peak hours.

  • International Security standards : The security system of the building has been planned keeping in mind requirements of reputed companies based on local and international information relating to perceived crime and terror threats in the region, with complete segregation between public and occupier areas.
